Usage: spiegel --type=type --url=couchdb-url options

  --type=type             The type of the process: update-listener, change-listener or replicator

  --url=couchdb-url       The URL to the CouchDB instance

Options:

  --batchSize=size        The maximum number of updates that will be processed in a batch.
                          Default: 100.

  --batchTimeout=timeout  The time in milliseconds to wait after an update before the batch is
                          considered done regardless of whether there are any more updates.
                          Default: 1000.

  --log-level=level       error|warn|info|debug. Setting a particular level implies that all log
                          records at that level and above are logged.
                          Default: info.
